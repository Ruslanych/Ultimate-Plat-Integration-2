# Ultimate Plat Integration

### The most precise portrayal of platformer difficulty in Geometry Dash.

It takes the placements of the two most popular platformer lists:

* **The Platformer List** - aka TPL, list of all hardest platformers
* **Pemonlist** - list of rated platformers only

And in addition:
* **Difficulty Chart** - platformer difficulty tier system

With all of this, you get full understanding of levels' difficulty and status of platformer levels. Feel free to explore them with us!
